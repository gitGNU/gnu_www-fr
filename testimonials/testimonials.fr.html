GNU Testimonials

Vous rendez-vous compte? Le logiciel libre n'est pas seulement un passe-temps pour les programmeurs, c'est une contribution des gens et des entreprises partout dans le monde. Des fabricants ornementaux de ruban, aux chercheurs, le logiciel libre aide des centaines de milliers de personne dans leur travail journalier. Son dévellopement continue à une vitesse considérable pour combler les besoins des utilisateurs dans tous les types d'applications.

The GNU Project has been a major influence on free software development for more than a decade, creating a myriad of important tools such as robust compilers, powerful text editors, and even a fully functional operating system. The project was started in 1984 by MIT programmer Richard Stallman to create a free, UNIX-like operating environment. Since then, thousands of programmers have joined in the effort to produce free, high quality software which is accessible to everyone.

These testimonials challenge the misconceptions that free software is impractical, unreliable, and unsupported.

  • Free Software is Reliable
  • Free Software is Supported
  • Why Open Source Software / Free Software (OSS/FS)? Look at the Numbers!

Other testimonials:

  • HIRLAM Consortium (meteorological research)
  • A database application for organ transplantation waiting lists and quality control
  • CAD/CAM Software
  • Research ships performing deep seismic acquisition
  • Publishing and music production
